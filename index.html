Privacy Policy
Plain language. No legal runaround.

Who we are

DeepCut is a personal tool built by Aether Ventures. We built it because we think people deserve something that actually takes them seriously.

What we collect

Your first name, last name, and email address. Your answers to the 50 assessment questions. Your intake responses. That's it.

What we do with it

We use your answers to generate your personal profile and results. Your email is how you get back to your results. Nothing else.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data. Ever. We don't share it with advertisers, third parties, or anyone else. We don't use it to market to you. Your results belong to you.

Where it's stored

Your data is stored in a private Sheet accessible only to Aether Ventures. It is not publicly accessible.

Deleting your data

Use the "Remove my info" option on the home screen or in your results and enter your email. We'll process your request and remove your data.

Questions

Reach us through aetherventure.com.
